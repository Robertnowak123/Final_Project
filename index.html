Brunch & Breakfast

With its copious canals, beautiful views and great things to do, Amsterdam is a fantastic city to visit. However, the most important is a good meal in the morning and that's where you can find our top 5 places to visit! Check it out below...

Staring at Jacob

Located in Amsterdam Oud-West, this brunch place serves delicious and hearty Western American-style breakfast meals from large pancakes to barbecue chicken, various omelettes and meat dishes - definitely a place to visit if you have large appetite in the morning and are sick of small portions offered at many places in Amsterdam :) - the place is even run by an American chef. Staring at Jacob offers a great, warm vibe as well as a nice location along the canal. Please be aware that the service might be a bit slow, but the wait is definitely worth it and you will not leave this place with feelings of guilt. Please make sure to come on time as spots fill up very quickly on Saturdays and Sundays!

Address: Jacob van Lennepkade 215, 1054 ZP Amsterdam

Phone: +31 (0) 20 223 7498

Opening Hours: Monday: 11-17h; Tuesday: Closed; Wednesday: Closed; Thursday: 11-17h, Friday: 11-17h, Saturday: 11-17h, Sunday: 10-17h

Omlegg

As the name already alludes, this great brunch place is all about omelettes and it even prouds itself with being the first omelettery in the Netherlands. Besides the omelettes on their menu, Omlegg creates a special Omelette every week to surprise their guests. You can expect anything, from Tuna Melt to Ratatouille and from Chicken Sateh to Bolognese. All made into a delicous Omelette that you probably will never forget! Please be aware that the wait can be quite long at this venue, so please make sure the account for at least 30 minutes of waiting time.

Little Collins

Located in trendy Amsterdam Pijp area, Little Collins offers you a rather elegant brunch experience. Mixing traditional Dutch bread-based breakfasts with some Australian flavors (the place is run by a Melbourne chef), you can expecy your typical breakfast to be more fancy than usual while still being fairly priced. Whether it is oysters on bruschetta, Korean beef tartar or sundried cauliflower, you will come out of this place and feel like a better person! Just because this place is so popular, it is difficult to get a spot there on Saturdays and Sundays. So make sure to arrive at least 30 minutes before your breakfast!

Address: Jacob van Lennepkade 215, 1054 ZP Amsterdam

Phone: +31 (0) 20 223 7498

Oriental City

For all of those of you who are sick of typical Western breakfast & brunch cuisine, Oriental City serves mouth-watering Chinese breakfast dishes. Located in the heart of Amsterdam's chinatown (about a 10 minute walk from Centraal Station), this venue offers you a very authentic Chinese "Dim Sum" breakfast experience including various types of dumplings and other Chinese specialties. Please be aware that the wait can be quite long at this venue, so please make sure the account for at least 30 minutes or even an hour of waiting time. However, you can check out the historic Redlight District right around the corner in the meantime..
